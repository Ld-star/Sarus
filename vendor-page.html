FOR MARKET DATA VENDORS

For companies with proprietary market data, we provide solutions to help monetize your data and make it readily accessible to companies of any size - fast.

We host your reference data and make it available to traders, analysts, developers, data scientists and office staff with our range of professional solutions. With Monitor+, you can have your market data product ready and available in a short time and with minimal effort.

Delivering your data via the Monitor+ API can integrate your data into your customers trading systems, ensuring their continued use of your data for the future.

Our solutions work for business of all size and enable customer to analyse and use market data in new ways to enhance their understanding of the market to give them commercial advantage.

We provide vendors with the metrics they need to see exactly how their data is being used. These metrics help vendors to understand more about how customers are using their data and can be analysed to give editorial, products and sales colleagues invaluable market insight.




Sarus provides the analytic solutions that your customers need and takes care of all the technology, leaving you to focus on your business and customers. We support time series data and metadata and can control individual user access to datasource or data category level. We'll create the tools to enable you to create and update your database on our servers. Your data will then be available only to your registered customers.
